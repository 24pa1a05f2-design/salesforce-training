# About Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps businesses manage customers, sales, services, marketing, and business operations efficiently.

It allows companies to:
- Store customer information
- Track communication and sales
- Improve customer service
- Automate business processes
- Build custom applications

Salesforce is widely used by organizations around the world because of its flexibility, scalability, and cloud-based architecture.
